What is grid based design?

Grid based design is laying out the content of your webpage onto a grid consisting of columns and rows.

Whats all the hype about responsive webpages?

Nowadays people are using different kind of devices to access the internet and it has become important to build web pages in a way that they could be shown the same as on a mobile phone and your laptop

What is semantic structure?

Semantics is the study of the meanings of words and phrases in a language. Semantic elements = elements with a meaning. A semantic element clearly describes its meaning to both the browser and the developer.
